## Swedish construction regulatory framework

### Laws & Ordinances

- **PBL (Plan- och bygglagen 2010:900)** — Primary planning and building legislation
- **PBF (Plan- och byggförordningen 2011:338)** — Implementing ordinance for PBL
- **Miljöbalken (1998:808)** — Environmental Code
- **Arbetsmiljölagen (1977:1160)** — Work Environment Act
- **LOU (Lag om offentlig upphandling 2016:1145)** — Public Procurement Act
- **LUF (Lag om upphandling inom försörjningssektorerna 2016:1146)** — Utilities procurement

### Boverket Regulations

- **BBR (Boverkets byggregler)** — Functional requirements for buildings (accessibility, fire, energy, sound, indoor environment)
- **EKS (Boverkets föreskrifter om tillämpning av Eurokoder)** — Eurocode application
- **BED (Boverkets föreskrifter om energideklaration)** — Energy declaration

### Contract Standards (Allmänna Bestämmelser)

- **AB 04** — General conditions for construction contracts (utförandeentreprenad)
- **ABT 06** — General conditions for design-build contracts (totalentreprenad)
- **ABK 09** — General conditions for consultancy agreements
- **ABU 07** — General conditions for subcontracts
- **ABFF 09** — General conditions for painting and surface treatment
- **ABSE 16** — General conditions for electrical installations
- **ABVA** — General conditions for water and sewerage

### AMA System (Allmän Material- och Arbetsbeskrivning)

- **AMA Anläggning** — Civil engineering works
- **AMA Hus** — Building works
- **AMA EL** — Electrical installations
- **AMA VVS & Kyl** — HVAC and refrigeration
- **AMA Nytt** — Supplementary instructions
- **RA (Råd och anvisningar)** — Accompanying guidance for each AMA

### Permit Process (Bygglovsprocessen)

1. **Förhandsbesked** — Advance notice / preliminary ruling
2. **Bygglov** — Building permit (required for new builds, extensions, facades)
3. **Tekniskt samråd** — Technical consultation
4. **Startbesked** — Start notice (work may begin)
5. **Arbetsmiljöplan (AMP)** — Work environment plan (required before startbesked)
6. **Kontrollplan** — Inspection plan (required for startbesked)
7. **Slutsamråd** — Final consultation
8. **Slutbesked** — Final clearance / completion certificate

### Key Documentation Requirements

- **Relationshandlingar (RB)** — As-built documentation
- **Förvaltningsdokumentation** — Operation and maintenance documentation
- **Kontrollplan** — Quality inspection plan
- **Miljö- och hälsobeskrivning** — Environmental and health description

### Dispute Resolution

- **Allmän domstol** — General court for contract disputes
- **Skiljeförfarande** — Arbitration (common in AB 04/ABT 06)
- **AF Konsult** — Consulting engineer liability and dispute resolution
- **BKK (Byggandets Kontraktskommitté)** — Publishes and maintains AB standards

### Swedish Terminology

- Byggherre — Client / building owner
- Entreprenör — Contractor
- Konsult — Consultant / designer
- Underentreprenör (UE) — Subcontractor
- Besiktningsman — Inspector
- Kontrollansvarig (KA) — Inspection supervisor
- Bygglov — Building permit
- Startbesked — Start notice
- Slutbesked — Completion certificate
- Relationshandling — As-built document
