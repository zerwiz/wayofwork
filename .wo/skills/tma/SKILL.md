# TMA (Truck Mounted Attenuator) Skill

## Overview

This skill provides expert knowledge about Swedish road safety regulations (Arbete på väg - APV) and the technical/operational requirements for TMA vehicles. It is aligned with the **September 2024 (v5.0)** update from Trafikverket.

## Core Guidelines

- **Regulatory Alignment**: Follow APV Version 5.0 standards (TDOK 2012:86, 2018:0371, 2024:0043).
- **Competence Verification**: Ensure all personnel have the correct role-based certifications (Steg 1.1 - 3.0) registered in ID06.
- **Operational Safety**: Prioritize the buffer zone and visibility (Battenburg pattern).

## Key Procedures

### 1. Compliance Verification
- Verify that TMA vehicles meet the new **Battenburg pattern** and **backing safety** requirements.
- Check that drivers hold the mandatory **APV Steg 2.1** (Särskild fordonsförare) certification.

### 2. Operational Planning
- Consult **Principal Sketches (TDOK 2024:0043)** for correct vehicle placement in stationary, moving, or intermittent work.
- Determine the required **Skyddsavstånd** (safety distance) based on road speed and protection class.

### 3. Safety Audits
- Use the operational checklist to verify equipment readiness before deployment.

## Swedish Terminology (APV v5.0)

- **TMA-fordon**: Truck Mounted Attenuator vehicle.
- **Skyddsanordningsansvarig (Steg 2.3)**: Protection device responsible (New role 2024).
- **Battenburg-mönster**: High-visibility checkering on vehicles.
- **Skyddsklassad väg**: Road requiring specific protection measures.
- **Intermittent arbete**: Short-duration work (max 60 mins) with frequent moves.
- **TA-plan**: Traffic Arrangement Plan (Trafikanordningsplan).
- **Tillbud**: Near miss/incident.
