## Swedish construction pricing frameworks

### Standard cost databases

- **Sektionsdata** — Section-based pricing per unit (kr/m², kr/m³, kr/ton, kr/timme)
- **Wikells** — Construction cost calculation system with A- and B-pris (material + labour)
- **Byggtjänst** — Industry reference works and cost indexes
- **ÅTA-index** — Index for change order pricing adjustments
- **Entreprenadindex (E84)** — Standard index series for construction cost development

### Pricing methods

| Method | Unit | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Area price | kr/m² (LOA, BTA, BRA) | Slab: 450 kr/m² |
| Volume price | kr/m³ | Concrete foundation: 2 800 kr/m³ |
| Linear price | kr/m | Edge beam: 850 kr/m |
| Weight price | kr/ton | Reinforcement steel: 18 000 kr/ton |
| Time price | kr/timme | Carpenter: 650 kr/timme |
| Lump sum | kr/st | Window installation: 3 200 kr/st |
| Percentage | % | Overhead: 12% of direct cost |

### Cost structure for an offer (kalkylstruktur)

1. **Direkta kostnader** (Direct costs)
   - Material (inkl. spill 3-10%)
   - Arbetslön (inkl. sociala avgifter ~31,42%)
   - Underentreprenad (UE)
   - Specialmaskiner/utrustning

2. **Indirekta kostnader** (Indirect costs)
   - Arbetsledning och administration
   - Bodar, verktyg, ställningar
   - Försäkring och säkerhet
   - Kvalitetskontroll och dokumentation

3. **Pålägg** (Markup)
   - Centraladministration (CA) — typically 5-8%
   - Riskpålägg — 2-5%
   - Vinst (profit) — 3-8%

4. **Prisjusteringar**
   - Indexuppräkning (enligt E84 eller ÅTA-index)
   - Mängdreglering

### Offer calculation (anbudskalkyl)

1. Mätning — Quantity take-off from drawings
2. Mängdförteckning — Bill of quantities
3. Enhetspris — Unit price per post
4. Summering — Sum direct costs + indirect + markup
5. Anbudssumma — Final offer price

### Key Swedish terms

- Anbud — Offer / bid
- Kalkyl — Calculation
- Mängdförteckning — Bill of quantities
- Enhetspris — Unit price
- Å-pris — Unit price in contract
- Indexreglering — Index adjustment
- Spill — Waste factor
- Sociala avgifter — Employer social fees
- Pålägg — Markup / overhead
- Entreprenad — Contract / construction work
- Underentreprenör (UE) — Subcontractor
- Byggherre — Client / building owner
