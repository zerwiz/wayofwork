## Swedish construction safety

Regulated by **Arbetsmiljöverket** through **AFS** (Arbetsmiljöverkets författningssamling).

## Key AFS regulations

- **AFS 2023:10** — Building and civil engineering work
- **AFS 2001:1** — Systematic work environment management
- **AFS 2020:1** — Personal protective equipment
- **AFS 2012:2** — Working at heights
- **AFS 2019:3** — Machinery and technical devices

## Skyddsrond (safety round)

Regular inspections:
- Weekly by foreman
- Monthly with worker representative
- Quarterly with management

## BAS-P / BAS-U

- **BAS-P** — Principal for planning and design (projektering)
- **BAS-U** — Principal for execution (utförande)
- Required by AFS on all construction sites

## Required documentation

- Risk assessment (riskbedömning) before new work tasks
- Safety inspection reports (skyddsrondsprotokoll)
- Incident/near miss reports (tillbudsrapport)
- Work environment plan (arbetsmiljöplan — AMP)

## Swedish terms

- Arbetsmiljö — Work environment
- Skyddsrond — Safety inspection
- Tillbud — Near miss / incident
- Riskbedömning — Risk assessment
- Fallskydd — Fall protection
- Byggarbetsmiljösamordnare — Construction W/E coordinator
