Guidelines for creating formal incident reports (Tillbud/Olycka) in compliance with the Swedish Work Environment Authority (Arbetsmiljöverket).
</description>

## Requirements for Incident Reports

When generating or reviewing an incident report, ensure the following information is captured:

1.  **Typ av händelse (Type of event):**
    *   *Tillbud (Near miss):* An event that could have led to an injury.
    *   *Olycka (Accident):* An event that led to personal injury.
    *   *Allvarligt tillbud/olycka:* A serious event that MUST be reported to Arbetsmiljöverket immediately.

2.  **Tid och plats (Time and Location):**
    *   Exact date and time.
    *   Specific location on the construction site (e.g., "Block C, 3rd floor scaffolding").

3.  **Händelseförlopp (Description of the event):**
    *   What happened?
    *   What was the person doing before the event?
    *   What equipment or materials were involved?

4.  **Orsaksanalys (Root Cause Analysis):**
    *   Why did it happen? (e.g., lack of training, faulty equipment, stress, missing safety gear).

5.  **Åtgärder (Mitigation / Actions):**
    *   *Omedelbara åtgärder:* Immediate actions taken to secure the site.
    *   *Förebyggande åtgärder:* Long-term actions to prevent recurrence (e.g., update risk assessment, new routines).

## AI Agent Instructions
*   Always maintain a serious and objective tone.
*   If an event sounds like an "Allvarligt tillbud" (Serious near miss) or "Allvarlig olycka" (Serious accident), you **MUST** remind the user that it needs to be reported to Arbetsmiljöverket within 24 hours.
*   Format reports using clear Markdown headers and bullet points.
